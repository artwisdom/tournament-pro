Rivvl — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026
Effective: May 28, 2026

Who we are

Rivvl is published by Andromeda Kinship LLC (Fall River, Massachusetts, USA). Contact: support@andromedakinship.com.

What we collect

Rivvl stores your data locally on your device using iOS's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage). We do not maintain a server-side copy of your tournaments, brackets, match results, settings, or any data you input.

The only data that leaves your device is:

  • Purchase records — when you subscribe or restore purchases, RevenueCat (our subscription management provider) receives a purchase token from Apple's StoreKit. RevenueCat uses this to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

What we do with it

Locally-stored data stays on your device and is used solely to render the app's screens and persist your tournaments, brackets, and match results between sessions. Purchase data is used only to gate premium features behind your active subscription.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not show you ads.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that profile individual users.

Your rights

Because Rivvl stores your data locally, you can delete all of it at any time by using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings or by uninstalling the app. This permanently removes all tournaments, brackets, scores, and preferences from your device.

For purchase data held by RevenueCat or Apple, refer to their respective privacy policies for deletion and access rights.

Children

Rivvl is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how data flows in Rivvl, we'll update this page and note the change in the app's release notes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@andromedakinship.com
